Course Details

In 2008, Ron Pritchard completed a renovation of the Bayou DeSiard Country Club Golf Course, thus recapturing many of the original Perry Maxwell image with small, rolling, undulating greens. Other upgrades include new 419 Bermuda fairway grass and MiniVerdeTM greens and some newly added and rebuilt, laser leveled tee boxes enhancing the overall design. Also, the course was lengthened by 350 yards to play at 7234 yards from the championship tees. We feel these changes are very positive and will create an exciting challenge for all contestants. There will be live scoring during the tournament and electronic scoreboards and strategic locations on the course so that spectators can keep up with the with the players on the course.
